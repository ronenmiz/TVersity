- (TVersityAFDeviceStatus) currentStatus
readatomicassign

Device current status. Notice this value does not update dynamically. It represents the status of the device at the moment this instance of the object was created. To get real time updates on a device status, register to the TVersityAFEventPlayerStatusChange or TVersityAFEventServerStatusChange events.
